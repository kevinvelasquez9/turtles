Can you last until 2023?

Play as a turtle fighting for survival in a world polluted with plastic waste. Your journey starts from the beginning of plastic production, which began in the mid-20th century, and goes through the years until 2023. With each passing year, the amount of plastic waste increases, making survival even more challenging. Your ultimate goal is to survive until 2023, but be warned, this is no easy feat. Do you have what it takes to make it? Use the arrow keys to begin!
